## 1. Go-to-Market Strategy

### Campaign Name: **"We Missed You, Champion"**

The strategy is built on one insight from ARIA's research: customers don't leave PulseRetail angry — they leave unnoticed. Our entire GTM approach weaponises that insight. We're not going to blast them with discounts. We're going to make them feel seen, valued, and part of something.

**Three-Phase Launch:**

**Phase 1 — Soft Launch (Week 1–2):** Email the top 20% most-engaged past customers (those who opened at least one email). Introduce PulsePass as an "early access" privilege. No promotion, just belonging. "You're one of the first to see this."

**Phase 2 — Activation (Week 3–4):** Full email list activation. Double-points week. Social proof wave — Instagram Stories from real members using the portal. Micro-influencer seeding (5 Irish fitness creators, 10K–80K following).

**Phase 3 — Always-On (Month 2+):** PulsePass becomes the permanent post-purchase destination. Every order confirmation links directly to the portal. Automated trigger emails fire at Days 3, 7, 14, 30 post-purchase.

## 2. Brand Messaging Framework

**Core Message:** *"PulseRetail knows you."*

| Audience | Message | Tone |
|---|---|---|
| Yoga buyers | "Your practice deserves more than a kit." | Calm, mindful, aspirational |
| Gym/HIIT buyers | "Every rep earns a reward." | Punchy, energetic, direct |
| Running buyers | "Your next mile starts here." | Motivational, goal-focused |
| Lapsed (30+ days) | "Your Pulse never stopped. Come back." | Warm, no-pressure, human |

**Brand Voice Guardrails:**
- Never: pushy, discount-first, generic
- Always: personal, earned, activewear-smart
